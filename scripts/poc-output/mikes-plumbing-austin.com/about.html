About Mike's Plumbing

Our Story

Mike Johnson started Mike's Plumbing in 2012 after 8 years working for large plumbing corporations. He wanted to build a company that actually cared about customers — no hidden fees, no upselling, just honest work at fair prices. Today, Mike's team of 6 licensed plumbers serves the entire greater Austin area.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be Austin's most trusted plumber by showing up fast, diagnosing correctly, and fixing it right the first time.
