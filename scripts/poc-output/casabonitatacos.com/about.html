About Casa Bonita Tacos

Authentic Mexican Flavors Since 2009

Our Story

In 2009, Maria and Roberto Gutierrez bet everything on a dream — an authentic Mexican restaurant in Denver's Baker neighborhood. Roberto's grandmother Esperanza had spent 40 years perfecting the recipes in Oaxaca, and Roberto spent 5 years as a chef in Mexico City before bringing those flavors to Colorado. Today, their children work alongside them, and Esperanza's recipes are still the heart of every dish.

Our Mission

To bring the soul of authentic Mexican home cooking to Denver — one taco at a time — and to make every guest feel like family.
