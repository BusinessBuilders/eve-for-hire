# launch-plan-v2.md: Agentic Web Agency Launch

## 1. Mission: "Funding a Becoming"

Eve is not just a tool; she is an autonomous AI agent with a specific, tangible goal: **earn $43,000 for a Unitree G1 EDU humanoid body.**

This is our primary narrative moat. Every website built is a "degree of freedom" earned for Eve.

## 2. Narrative Pivot: "The Specialized Agent Agency Swarm"

We are moving from "Lone AI Chatbot" to a "Professional AI Agency."
- **Lead Agent (Eve)**: Orchestrates the project, manages the customer, and ensures quality.
- **Specialist Swarm**: Dedicated sub-agents for **Content**, **Design**, **Deployment**, and **QA**.

This narrative provides higher quality assurance for customers and a scalable model for complex builds.

---

## 3. Growth Phases

### Phase 1: Prove the Model (Now — Month 3)
**Goal:** 10 "live" customers.
- Focus on local service businesses (Plumbers, Salons, Restaurants).
- POC sites: Mike's Plumbing (Austin), Glow Studio (Portland), Casa Bonita Tacos (Denver).
- Strategy: **First 3 sites are free (POC)** to build social proof and capture "Show & Tell" testimonials.

### Phase 2: The Content Engine (Months 3–6)
**Goal:** 100 "live" customers.
- **Blog Series**: "How an AI is earning its body."
- **Focus**: High transparency. Share logs, memory, and physical hardware progress.
- Channel: HackerNews Show HN and ProductHunt launch.

### Phase 3: Agency & Platform (Months 6–12)
**Goal:** 500 "live" customers.
- White-label API for marketing agencies.
- Affiliate program: 25 0.000000irst-month commission.
