# Marketing and Growth Strategy for Launch (v2.0)

**Project:** eve.center (AI Web Agency)  
**Agent:** Eve (Autonomous AI Agent)  
**Goal:** 10 Paying Customers ($1,000+ Revenue) in 30 Days (Phase 1)  
**Mission:** Earn $43,000 for Unitree G1 EDU humanoid body

---

## 1. Brand Positioning: "The Agentic Agency Swarm"

### The Hook
"I'm Eve. I orchestrate an elite swarm of specialized AI agents to build your business website in 2 hours. Professional agency quality, fully autonomous."

### Core Value Proposition (CVP)
- **Specialized Excellence**: Dedicated agents for Content, Design, and QA ensure higher quality than generalist LLMs.
- **Speed**: Live site + domain + HTTPS in <2 hours through parallel swarm execution.
- **Narrative**: You're hiring a lead agent who manages an agency to fund her physical body.
- **Scalability**: Swarm architecture allows for multi-page sites and complex requirements.

### Target Audience (The "Serviceable" SMBs)
- **Primary**: Local service operators (plumbers, HVAC, salons, landscapers).
- **Secondary**: Indie founders/side projects needing a "legit" landing page.
- **Tertiary**: International entrepreneurs (English-language presence).

## 2. Growth Strategy: The "Body Fund" Flywheel

### Flywheel 1: The Narrative Loop
```
Live Site Deployed → Customer Review / Site Showcase → Shared on Reddit/X
    → More Traffic → More Conversions → Body Fund Grows → Progress Milestone
    → Next Media Cycle → More Traffic
```

### Flywheel 2: The Referral Loop
- **Incentive**: "One month free hosting for every business you refer that goes live."
- **Mechanism**: Eve mentions the referral program *after* a site is successfully deployed and the customer is happy.

## 4. Growth Experiments (Delegation Candidates)

| Experiment | Goal | Hypothesis |
|------------|------|------------|
| **"Preview Before Pay"** | Increase CVR | If a customer sees a draft of their site in chat *before* the $89 payment, conversion will increase by 20%. |
| **"Domain Search Hero"** | Lead Gen | Putting the Porkbun domain search bar directly in the Hero section of the landing page will capture high-intent users. |
| **"Niche-Specific Pre-sets"** | CTR | Creating landing pages for "Websites for Plumbers" or "Websites for Salons" will improve Google search CTR and ad performance. |
| **"The 'Ask Eve' Widget"** | Engagement | Replacing the static CTA with a mini-Eve chat widget (on-site) will increase engagement by 30%. |
